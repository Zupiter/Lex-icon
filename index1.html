Motivation

English in today's world is no longer a way of communication but is turning out to be a need of the present. Without the knowledge of the English Language, it is becoming difficult to survive because it is now the language of science, aviation, computers, diplomacy, education, multinational communication, tourism or simply the language of everything.


Vision

Keeping in mind the importance of English in modern world, Lex-icon aims to provide a platform that would offer a great opportunity to enhance vocabulary skills in English based on textual learning. This would help carry forward the idea of 'Education for all' and would also help in improving the English literacy percentage of present India.

About the App


Lex-icon is an open platform where one can log in to enhance vocabulary skills in English Language. It has been categorized into three sections:

The Word of the day section -- It updates the users with a new word everyday.

The Trending section -- It helps users to upload new word posts that would be visible to all the members of the Lex-icon family.

The Favourites section -- This section is a listing of all the word posts liked by the logged in user by that time.

FAQ


Q: Where would I find the best app experience?
A: The app works best on desktops.

Q: What is the purpose of this app? / What can I do using this app?
A: Get to learn new english words, their meanings and usage every now and then and also add new ones.

Q: What should I do to see the word posts?
A: If already have an account, sign in and if not then sign up to create one.

Q: What is the blur image concept on 'Sign Up' page?
A: Blur image concept is related to password strength. Stronger the password, clearer the image.

Q: How can I add a word that I found was new?
A: Log in to find a word submission form at the top under 'Trending' section.

Q: What should I do if I found a word interesting and I want to see it again in future?
A: Simply click on the like button provided under each word post and go to favourites tab to see all your favourites.
